Composite Skilled Lab

A Composite Skill Lab is an innovative initiative mandated by the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) to enhance skill-based education in schools across India. These labs are designed to provide students with hands-on training in various fields, bridging the gap between theoretical learning and real-world application. We provide a holistic solution for complete lab setup.

FAQ

Curious about how AI-Robo can benefit your child or school? Below are answers to some common questions to help you understand our offerings and enrollment process better.

What is AI-Robo?

AI-Robo is a STEAM-based institute focused on providing hands-on education in Robotics, AI, and IoT, aimed at developing 21st-century skills among young learners.

Why Robotics and AI for Children?

Introducing children to Robotics and AI fosters essential skills like problem-solving, creativity, and adaptability, preparing them to thrive in a tech-driven future.

What are 21st-Century Skill Sets?

21st-century skills include critical thinking, digital literacy, collaboration, and adaptability—skills necessary for success in modern, technology-rich environments.

What is AI and IoT?

AI (Artificial Intelligence) enables machines to learn and make decisions, while IoT (Internet of Things) connects everyday devices to the internet, enhancing functionality and automation.
